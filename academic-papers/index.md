# Academic Papers Portfolio

This directory contains a curated selection of academic papers from my MSW curriculum. These pieces are selected for their contribution to the social work profession, depth of literature review, and practical utility.

---

## Curated Papers

* ### [🧬 The Conceptual Blueprint: Theories, Models, and Perspectives](./theories-models-perspectives.md)
  An objective analysis clarifying the structural boundaries between social work frameworks, clinical models, and conceptual lenses.

* ### [🏛️ The Architecture of Social Construction: Evaluating Racial Bias in Welfare Reform](./welfare-reform-bias.md)
  A macro-level critical review evaluating how racial bias, weaponized tropes, and subjective social constructions historically motivated punitive welfare reform legislation.

* ### [⛪ Strategic Briefing: Gender Equity and Organizational Inclusion in Faith-Based Non-Profits](./faith-based-inclusion.md)
  An organizational analysis tracking the underrepresentation of female leadership in faith-based non-profit structures, highlighting strategies for incremental institutional change.

* ### [🏠 Structural Determinants of Well-Being: Affordable Housing Infrastructure as Public Health](./housing-health-determinants.md)
  A macro-level analysis evaluating affordable housing shortages and commercial property ownership trends as critical drivers of community health inequities.

* ### [⚖️ The Analytical Scope of Critical Practice: An Evaluative Critique of Intersectionality Theory](./intersectionality-theory-critique.md)
  An academic evaluation of intersectionality theory assessing its structural parsimony, philosophical soundness, and empirical limitations within systemic practice.

* ### [🎓 Mentorship as a Catalyst: Exploring Support Systems in Graduate Training](./graduate-mentorship-support-systems.md)
  A qualitative exploration examining how emerging professional scholars experience institutional mentorship and structured support networks within higher education settings.
